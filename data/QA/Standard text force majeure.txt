Standard force majeure text, if a student decides to go to the BoE:

In that case, email dacs-boe@maastrichtuniversity.nl with me in CC. Please prepare your case in detail. Don’t was the BoE’s time. You need to mention the following:
- Name, program (DSAI or CS), course, group number.
- Which sessions on what dates you will miss.
- Why you miss them + sufficient proof (document/scans/doctor’s note, etc.)
- Confirmation that you will still support your team online and that you’re committed to the project.
